Pressly | Toronto | Full-time+Onsite | https://www.pressly.com

Pressly is a platform for helping professionals manage communications at scale. From an arch perspective, think of our product as a modern discussion forum + mailing list that keeps groups better connected and informed. We have clients like ScotiaBank, Deloitte, Rogers, MongoDB, and many others improving how they build communities (internal+external) and distribute information across a network of collaborators.

Our stack is Go on the backend, JS (ES6+flow) / Reactjs / Mobx on the frontend and React Native for our mobile apps (iOS/Android). We do a lot of open source work too, see https://github.com/pressly and https://upper.io.
