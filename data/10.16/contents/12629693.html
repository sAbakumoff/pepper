Sourcegraph is creating the best way to read and understand code.

As a programmer, you spend hours every day trying to answer simple questions like "How do I use this library?", "Does this function already exist?", and "What's going on in this changeset?" Sourcegraph makes exploring the world's code as painless as searching and browsing the web.

About us –

We're a small team founded by Stanford and Palantir alums. Our users and customers span programmers across the world, influential open-source authors, and major companies like Twitter. We're working on technical challenges like scaling code analysis and search to every codebase in the world.
