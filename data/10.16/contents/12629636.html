Robin | Boston, MA / Remote | Full Time

Robin is a better way to manage meeting rooms. It's a bit like having a search engine (eg. OpenTable) for your office -- you can find and book rooms based on the stuff you need, remove no-show meetings automatically, and get reports on how the office is actually being used. Netflix, Sonos, and KAYAK all use it to power their conference rooms. We’re looking for engineers that get things done for the following roles:

* Mobile Engineer

* Platform Engineer

* QA Engineer

* Frontend Engineer

* Infrastructure And DevOps Engineer

The interview process consists of a few phone screens and an in-person interview. Job descriptions and applications at https://angel.co/robin/jobs
